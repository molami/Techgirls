Xiao Zhan (English name: Sean Xiao) is a Chinese actor and singer. He is a member of the male idol group X NINE. Xiao received recognition for his roles in the dramas "Oh! My Emperor" and "The Untamed". Xiao worked as a photographer and graphic designer prior to his debut. In 2015, Xiao participated in the reality program "X-Fire" where he trained to be an idol alongside 15 other trainees.

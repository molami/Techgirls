Wang Yi Bo is a Chinese singer, actor, dancer, TV host, model, and motorcycle racer. He is a member of the South Korean-Chinese boyband UNIQ. He attended high school in South Korea and is fluent in both Chinese and Korean. In 2011, during his second year in high school, Wang participated in the IBD dance competition, emerging as the top sixteen in the hip-hop category.

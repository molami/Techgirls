Who is Wan Peng

Wan Peng is a Chinese actress under Easy Entertainment. She attended the Beijing Dance Academy. In 2018, Wan made her debut in the coming-of-age youth drama "When We Were Young", for which she received positive reviews and became known for her role. She received the Best Newcomer award at the China News Entertainment Awards and the Outstanding Actress award at the China Internet Radio and Video Convention. Wan then played supporting roles in the period detective drama 'Detective L" and medical rescue drama "Rush Into Danger".
